    More About Me

    Being born in the late 90's, I am no stranger to the web. In watching the internet grow, I have always had a passion for the design aspect, and how much the overall aesthetic and standards, aswell as trends of webpages/applications have changed.

    For over a decade, I have been in some form intertwined with aspects of web design, whether it be doing graphic design in my teen years, to professional logo design later on. Recently however I have dived into the world of web development, and learning the back/front end of things to put my design enthusiasm to more effective use.

    I have currently completed freelance website design projects, aswell as various HTML and CSS challenges. I am eager to branch out into the world of web development, and hope this website aswell as my professional portfolio can capture and display my passion for the world wide web.
